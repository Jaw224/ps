About GoPED

GoPED aims to make Pittsburgh more accessible for it's residents. With a fantastic bike infrastructure, Pittsburgh is a great place to get around on a bike. Electric bikes make your daily commute traffic and parking free.


Why GoPED?

  • - Go further, faster compared to a traditional bike
