Travel

Highlining Hijinks - May 2018


While I was in college I became interested in slacklining. People in the Pittsburgh slacklining community regularly meet up with the greater slacklining community in surrounding states for to highline. This article is about when me and some friends went down to West Virginia for some highlining fun.

Pacific Northwest Trip - June 2018


My family took a vacation to the Pacific Northwest during the summer of 2018. It was the first time I got to visit Portland and Seattle. We stopped at Mount Rainer National Park for a few days on the way from Portland to Seattle. I wrote a series of blog posts, one for each day of the trip.

Running

Bel Monte 50k Ultramarathon - March 2018


In March of 2018 I competed in an Ultramarathon trail running race in the Blue Ridge Mountains of Virginia. 17 miles into the race I had to drop out because of complications with my metabolic disorder. In this blog post I talk about how I got into trail running and the months, days, and hours leading up to my race.

Bitcoin Zine - December 2017


Many people wish that they knew more about Bitcoin and blockchain technology. I created this Zine to educate people from the ground up about the technology, economics, and sociopolitical impacts of a booming cyrptocurrency market.
