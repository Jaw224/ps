About Me

Thanks for checking out my site! I'm Alex. I recently graduated from the University of Pittsburgh where I studied Mechanical Engineering. I focused on Product Design and Entrepreneurship, getting a certificate in Innovation, Product Design, and Entrepreneurship. During my Senior year at Pitt I balnced training for an Ultramarathon, a full courseload, and my responsibilities to my floor and staff as a Resident Assistant.

Outside of work I enjoy spending my time outdoors, with trail running being my activity of choice. Since graduating I've been mountain biking in Pittsburgh and Philadelphia as a way of getting more time on the trails.

Starting in July I will be traveling in, and living out of a Mini-Van with my girlfriend Zoe. We plan to visit National Parks and cities along the west coast as we look for a new city to live in. Check back for more info about our travels!

I am currently available for project consultations and other work opportunities. Please feel free to reach out to me with any questions.
