Wildfire Research

During my 2018 travels throughout California the sweeping wildfires dessimated many acres of land and temporarily closed National Parks (including Yosemite.) During school I designed mechatronics projects using microcontrollers and various sensors. This project aims to bridge the gap between academia and wildland fire fighters to provide them with more comprehensive data and maps.

Bitcoin Zine

For the past decade Bitcoin and other cryptocurrencies have recieved sporatic and inconsistent media coverage. This, combined with the complexity of the technology, economics, and geopolitical complications of Bitcoin, leaves the general public with little digestable information. This Zine aims to simplify these complexities and provide easy to consume reading.

3D Printing Research

After getting my feet wet 3D printing in highschool, I was thrilled to accept a research position evaluating 3D printed lattice structures. Using an EOS 3D printer we were able to print Aluminum and Titanium lattice structures for testing. The goal of this research was to quantify heat transfer properties of varying 3D printed lattice structures. My report details the findings after a summer of experimental design and testing.
